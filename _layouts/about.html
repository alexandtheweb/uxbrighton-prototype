Who is UX Brighton aimed at?

You don’t need to be a "UX designer" to attend UX Brighton. We aim our events at designers, developers, project managers, entrepreneurs, copywriters and basically anyone who thinks that listening to users makes them better at their job.

About UX Brighton

UX Brighton is a community group disseminating knowledge in all aspects of user experience.

Founded in 2008 by Danny Hope.

We run regular meet-ups (these are always on the 2nd Tuesday of the month in central Brighton.

We also run an annual conference, usually in November.

We are experimenting with running special in-depth workshops.

Curate an event

Got an idea for a UX Brighton event you'd like to see? We'd love it if you wanted to curate an evening and put some speakers together! We have done this loads of times and can help you at every step.

We have a checklist to guide you through finding speakers, promoting the evening and running it on the night. We have a number of venues that we regularly use and can book for you. Drop us a line, join our Slack or send us a tweet and we'll get you started...
